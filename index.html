Designed for Audiophiles

Every feature is crafted to bring you closer to your music, offline or online.

Pure Engineering, Immersive Audio

Powered by media_kit hardware-accelerated playback pipeline. Enjoy low-latency playback of your FLAC, MP3, WAV, M4A, and AAC files without breaking a sweat.

Dynamically Personal UI

No more static players. SonicSnap analyzes your track's album art cover in real-time to generate a fluid matching color palette that styles the entire interface uniquely.

Dynamic Acoustics

Control your sound signature with our precision built-in multi-band Equalizer. Restores your customized presets automatically, ensuring your earphones always sound their best.

TWS & Smart Playback

Fully optimized for Bluetooth earphones and TWS buds. Connect and play immediately — SonicSnap recovers audio states seamlessly, even from cold starts.
